About Saad

Saad Mohammad Obaidullah is a prominent Bangladeshi student, entrepreneur, and advocate for digital learning. He is best known as the Founder and Managing Director of Student Portal BD, a digital platform aimed at revolutionizing education in Bangladesh through technology.

Personal Profile: Born in Joshati, Basail, Tangail (September 20, 2010 / Dec 20, 2011). Father Monnaf Ali (librarian, Bangladesh Shishu Academy), mother Karimun Nahar (homemaker). Currently resides in Tangail Town.

Education: Outstanding student at Bindubasini Government Boys High School (2023–present). Previously attended Nowabganj Model Government Primary School, Sristy Central School & College, Rajshahi, and Sristy Academic School. Fluent in Bangla, basic English, Hindi, Arabic (reading). Pursuing professional English at S@ifur’s English Academy.

Entrepreneurship: Founder of Student Portal BD — provides 2025 board textbooks, smart tools (reminder, digital notebook, dictionary, quizzes) and SPBD Quiz Championship 2026. Also manages Shadhin Bangla Store and authored "Computer and You" (50 TK) to spread tech literacy.

Achievements: 🥈 2nd place - National poster competition (10 Minute School). 🥈 2nd place - Quran recitation competition 2026 at school Milad Mahfil. Essay "The Impact of Social Media on Teenagers’ Mental Health". Skilled in Python, robotics, web basics, leadership, BNCC (2025), Red Crescent Youth (2023), Debate & Literature member.
